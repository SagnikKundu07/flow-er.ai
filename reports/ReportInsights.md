### Overview

* The highest grossing year was **2015**, with **$62M in Total Sales (Incl Tax)** and **$27M in Total Profit**, driven by approximately **71K sales transactions**
* In 2015, the **Southeast territory** contributed the highest **Total Sales (Excl Tax)**, accounting for **22.07%** of overall sales
* The **Great Lakes region** recorded the highest **Gross Margin** at **50.57%** across all regions
* The strongest **YoY growth** occurred in **April 2015**, reaching **23.88%** over the previous year

### Sales & Profit by Region

* Within the **Southwest territory**, **Texas** recorded the highest sales overall, with a **Yearly Avg Sales of $361.30K** in 2015
* Within the **Southeast territory**, **Florida** led in sales during 2015, while **Mississippi** and **Tennessee** showed significantly lower contributions, with yearly averages of **$38.20K** and **$35.02K** respectively
